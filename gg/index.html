Our Campus

DCT is located at One Central, next to Dubai World Trade Centre, students study in a purpose-built college facility at Dubai Tourism’s headquarters. This vibrant working environment will inspire and educate students while introducing them to the everyday practices of their chosen career. Through a variety of initiatives such as day trips, site visits, and guest lectures as well as our working internship programme, DCT provides a hands-on approach that is unrivalled within the industry.
